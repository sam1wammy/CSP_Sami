The oring of cheetahs

Cheetahs originated from North America, specifically in Texas, Nevada, and Wyoming. They eventually migrated to Euroasia and Africa, it is belived that cheetahs got there through the Bering Strait, which happened about 100,00 years ago, cheetahs eventually dispersed further south, which eventually ended in them setteling in Africa.
In ancieant times Cheetahs where considered valuable by royalty and nobles throughout Europe, Asia, and India

Later in the 1500s Cheetahs became popular for being hunting companions rivaled that to dogs, on the early 1900s cheetahs became a demand of sports like courinsg and leading their import into countries like India and Iran, which caused more diversity of Cheetahs aroound Asia. To this day cheetahs continue to be recognized by their speed, beauty, and consevation status, making their place permanent in the popular culture.

Cheetah fun facts!

You might think you know all this fun factsabout cheetahs but, did you always really understant them?. While we all know cheetahs are extreamly fast, they can go up to 50-80 mph making them one of the fastes land animal, their speed is caused by a combination of anatomical and physiological aadaptations specifically desinged for speed and agility. Cheetahs have an amazing vision, the sun doesn't affect their eyes that much thanks to their "tear lines" on bot sides of their nose. Cheetahs are large cats with unique sounds, Cheetas make various chirps, purrs, and hgih-pitched calls for communication, but cheetahs do not roar, which makes them look more like a cat than a thing that can kill you. Cheetahs are endangered species, there is less than 7,500 cheetahs left in the wild in Africa.
