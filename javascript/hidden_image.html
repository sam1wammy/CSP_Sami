Why are clouds important?

Clouds are important because they help make the Earth habitable. Clouds are crucial for Earth's climate, weather, and water cycle. Without clouds we would not have rain or any climate chang eat all and the Earth would not be habitable.

Find the Cloud!

Clouds are made up of tiny water droplets or ice crystals that form when water vapor in the air cools and condenses. They play a vital role in regulating Earth's temperature and weather patterns.
