Little Sahara

Why you should visit Little Sahara

Little Sahara is a unique desert landscape located in the heart of Utah. It offers stunning views, thrilling sand dunes, and a chance to experience the beauty of nature. Whether you're into hiking, photography, or just relaxing in a beautiful setting, Little Sahara has something for everyone. Little sahara is also a great get-away fro a weekend since it's located in Juab County, Utah and is 1hr and 11min away from Provo.
